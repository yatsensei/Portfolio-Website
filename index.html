01 — About

I'm Akein Tsung, a Bachelor of Artificial Intelligence student at the University of Technology Sydney. I'm less interested in models that work in a notebook and more interested in systems that work end-to-end — the data pipeline, the training run, the API, and everything that has to hold together for a model to actually be useful to someone.
